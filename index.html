Pluggable Transports
keep your tools connected

Transports provide ways to protect your data from aggressive censorship techniques, and can be included in a variety of tools.

We will be launching a broad, outward facing community here to expand the usage of transports and the community of implementers and developers.

How do I get involved?

Pluggable Transports Launch Meeting

Alongside FOCI, we will have an informal meeting to discuss the existing challenges in the space and map out areas where collaboration can advance a more free and open internet.
